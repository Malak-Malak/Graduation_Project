### **Supervisor Slots Workflow:**

###### 

###### **When a supervisor logs in for the first time they set their MaxTeams number**

###### **This number represents how many teams they can supervise at the same time**

###### **Supervisors can update their MaxTeams at any time**

###### **When a student views the supervisors list they can see:**

###### 

###### **Supervisor name**

###### **Max teams allowed**

###### **Current active teams count**

###### **Remaining slots**

###### 

###### 

###### **A supervisor with 0 remaining slots will not appear as available**
