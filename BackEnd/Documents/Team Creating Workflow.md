### **Team Creating Workflow:**



###### **A student who has no team can either:**

###### 

###### **Create a new team and choose a supervisor**

###### **Wait to be added to an existing team by another student**

###### 

###### 

###### **When creating a team:**

###### 

###### **Student enters the project title**

###### **Student sees a list of available supervisors with their remaining slots**

###### **Student picks a supervisor**

###### **Team is created with status Pending**

###### 

###### 

###### **The chosen supervisor receives the request and can approve or reject it**

###### **If approved → team status becomes Active, students can now access Kanban board**

###### **If rejected → team status becomes Rejected, student can create a new team**

###### **Only the team creator can add students to the team**

###### **Max 4 students per team**

###### **A student cannot be in more than one team at a time**
